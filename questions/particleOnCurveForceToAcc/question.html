Gravity $\vec{g} = -9.8\hat\jmath\rm\ m/s^2$ acts vertically. At the current instant the particle has $x = {{params.x_value}}\rm\ m$ and the force of the wire on the particle is $\vec{F}_w = {{params.Fw_vec}}\rm\ N$.

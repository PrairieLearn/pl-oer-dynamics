We will first find a function for $r(t)$. Fully solve for the constant of integration for $r(t)$ and provide $r(t)$ in terms of the scalar variable t only.

For symbolic inputs, do not enter decimals as part of the input. For example, if the answer is $2t$, enter $2t$ as 2*t, not 2.0*t.
